The Ultimate Kart Racing Experience

But after 11 years and the arrival of the Switch 2, it was finally time for a brand new entry: Mario Kart World.

The main innovation in Mario Kart World is its open world. All the tracks are now connected within one massive world you can freely explore in a dedicated free-roam mode. Races let you drive to and from each track, making the entire game feel like a single, connected experience. While this is a bold new direction, it does have a downside: the roads between tracks are often simple and not as exciting as the main courses. Thankfully, you can still choose to race just the tracks themselves if you prefer the classic experience.

Where Mario Kart World truly shines is in its gameplay. The controls and mechanics are so fun and responsive that going back to Mario Kart 8 feels impossible. Both new and returning tracks are a blast, and the visual presentation is more vibrant and charming than ever, thanks to the Switch 2's improved hardware.
