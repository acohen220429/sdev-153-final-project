An Epic Open-World Adventure

2017's Breath of the Wild was a milestone for the gaming industry, and a game I personally love. When a sequel was announced, I was beyond excitedâ€”and after years of anticipation, Tears of the Kingdom finally arrived in 2022. It absolutely delivered on my expectations.

While I still see Breath of the Wild as the more revolutionary title, Tears of the Kingdom is a more refined and polished experience in almost every way. The world is massive, yet filled with detail and surprises. The story, while sometimes simple, always inspires a sense of adventure. The new abilities open up an incredible amount of creativity, letting you approach challenges in ways that feel fresh and unique.

Although the world is largely the same as Breath of the Wild, the changes and additions make it feel new againâ€”and honestly, I couldn't resist the chance to explore this world I fell in love with all over again. Some people find the game too empty, but I think that sense of "emptiness" is part of its charm. There is always something to discover if you look for it, and the freedom to roam and experiment is unmatched. Tears of the Kingdom is a truly incredible video game.
